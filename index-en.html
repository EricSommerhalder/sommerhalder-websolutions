The realization
of your vision

Would you like to optimize your processes with a tailor-made app? Do you want to perfect your web presence with a unique, handmade website? Are you planning to add new functions to an existing product? With sound programming skills and a passion for creative solutions, I can turn your ideas into reality.

1.

First conversation

1.

First conversation

We start with a non-binding and free exchange. You tell me about your project and I make suggestions on how we can realize your ideas. There are no 'can'ts' with us - only tailor-made solutions!

2.

Design concept

2.

Concept

Before the actual implementation begins, we define together what your app or website should look like. Although I am not a designer myself, I can call in the right specialist from my network for this area if you wish, and together we will create a design that reflects your vision.

3.

Agreement

3.

Agreement

Together we define all the functions of your app or website and record them in detail. This results in a clear picture of the project and a structured schedule that helps us to maintain an overview and track the progress of the project.

4.

Project implementation

4.

Implementation

Now it's time to take your ideas from sketch to screen. You will receive regular updates on the progress and we can meet digitally or in person at any time to discuss any questions that arise.

5.

Project completion

5.

Completion

Your project will be successfully completed by the agreed date at the latest. I will provide you with the complete and carefully documented source code. If you need support with hosting or other questions, I will of course be at your disposal. Now you have the freedom to use your product independently.
